why digitally.world?



Digital : electronic digital devices; also : characterized by electronic and especially computerized technology.





As time passes, more people are exposed to the wonders of the internet at progressively younger ages. This is rational, considering society's quick transition to the digital world, but is not addressed enough. The majority of children with access to the internet do not know how to handle the risks and responsibilities that go hand in hand with it. Without this knowledge surges the risk of cyberbullying, sexual harassment, stolen identities, addiction the the internet, and insomnia, which in turn could cause anxiety, depression, eating disorders, and attention problems. Just like innovation and creative individuals, problems and problematic people are a normal part of the internet, but how one decides to deal with them is what matters.

Doubtless, technology advances at a mind-boggling speed and holds many benefits. But we as consumers do have to learn as we progress and advance of the pros and the cons of all that occurs so that true progress can bloom from here.




Broken down simply, to successfully integrate to the web, there are three necessary elements:


Level 1: Digital Citizenship

Digital Citizenship being "the ability to use digital technology and media in safe, responsible, and effective ways."


Level 2: Digital Creativity

The ability to become part of the digital ecosystem by creating new content and turning ideas into reality by using digital tools."


Level 3: Digital Entrepreneurship

"The ability to use digital media and technologies to solve global challenges or to create new opportunities."





What digitally.world intends to do is teach these concepts as fun and effectively as possible. This is a website created with the intention of reaching anyone in need of this information, from young children to professional adults.
